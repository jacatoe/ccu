Rock Icons

What Is An Icon?

Within any entertainment medium, there exist those who rise above the rest to leave a legacy not unlike that of a mythological god. In literature, there is Shakespeare,in film, Citizen Kane, in classical music, Beethoven. We call these people and/or their works, icons. While they may not be the absolute best at what they do (as it is very subjective), there is no arguing that icons like these are revolutionary for their time, and representative of their mediums. The rock 'n' roll genre of music is no different, as it holds many icons spanning most of its sub-genres, from the 1950s through today. While most rock icons are still alive and fresh in the minds of the world, it is certain that long after they are dead, they will be remembered for decades and maybe even centuries to come.
